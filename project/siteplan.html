NzillaStore Site Plan

Site Name: NzillaStore

NzillaStore is an online purchasing platform where customers can find and order affordable products easily. The name represents simplicity and accessibility.

Site Purpose

The purpose of NzillaStore is to:

  • Provide a user-friendly shopping experience for customers looking for affordable products.
  • Ensure smooth order placement and checkout process.

Scenarios

  • How do I find the best deals on products?
  • What steps are needed to place an order?
  • How can I track my purchase?

Color Schema

  • #1F3A47 (Dark Blue): Used for the header, footer, and navigation.
  • #D9782D (Burnt Orange): Used for price tags, buttons, and highlights.
  • #D3D3D3 (Light Gray): Background for product display sections.
  • #FFFFFF (White): Used for the main background.
  • #000000 (Black): Text and icons for readability.

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for headings.
  • Roboto: Used for body text.
